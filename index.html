About Us



Thamizh (Tamil) Power Solutions Private Limited, based in Chennai, is a leading provider of advanced power and energy solutions. With over 25 years of industry experience, we specialize in delivering high-performance systems including retrofit emission control devices (RECD), UPS systems, precision air conditioning (PAC),DRPC systems ( Dynamic Reactive Power Compensation), solar EPC projects, and electrical Installation works. Our team of experienced professionals ensures timely and high-quality deliveries, adhering to the highest industry standards. We are committed to enhancing power quality, improving energy efficiency, and minimizing environmental impact through innovative technologies and sustainable practices.
